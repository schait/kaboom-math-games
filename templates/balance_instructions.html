Balance It Out


Find the value of the black unknown weight. Make sure to keep the scale balanced!


Types of Objects

A positive weight. Will push one side of the scale down.
A balloon (negative weight). Will pull one side of the scale up.
The unknown (x). It can hold a weight (positive) or a balloon (negative). Find its value to solve the puzzle.
The opposite of the unknown (-x). It can also hold a weight or a balloon.
Actions

You can add an object to both sides. The scale will remain balanced.

You can remove objects from the scale.
  • But make sure to keep the scale balanced!
  • You can remove the same amount of weight from both sides, or remove weights that add up to 0.

You can combine weights on the same side, if none of them are unknown.

You can divide both sides by the same number.
