Equation Blaster


Shoot the enemy ships. Avoid enemy bullets and ships.


  • Press Space to shoot.
  • Press and hold S to activate shield. (But don't hold it for too long, or your shields will run out.)
  • Command Ships have equations on them that you need to solve.
    • When facing Command Ships, you may hide behind the wall on the bottom left for as long as you want while you solve the equation.
    • Once you solve the equation, enter your answer to select your ammo. You cannot shoot until you select ammo.
    • If you hit a Command Ship with the wrong ammo, it will shoot 3 fireballs at you which cannot be blocked by your shield. So be careful!
  • Score 1000 points to get an extra life.
